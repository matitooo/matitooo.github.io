Sarcasm Detection using a combination of LSTM, attention and manual features.

For the final project in the ANLP W.S. 2024 class, my group IsThisLoss and I developed LSTM and Bi-LSTM models with attention mechanisms and manually extracted linguistic features for sarcasm detection using the SemEval 2022 Task 6 dataset. The models were optimized through Bayesian hyperparameter tuning. Our best models achieved F1 scores of 0.264 and 0.262, highlighting the importance of incorporating external data and linguistic features.
